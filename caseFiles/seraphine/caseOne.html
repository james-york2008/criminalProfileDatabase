Dockside Cinder Event

  • Case Number:
    XM-284
  • Status:
    CLOSED
  • Date:
    10/12/25
  • Location:
    Virex Shipping Docks
  • Time:
    02:18 AM
  • Witnesses:
    4 dock workers

STOLEN OBJECT

A reinforced cargo container registered under private contract. Contents officially listed as “ceremonial metalwork.”

INCIDENT SUMMARY

At approximately 02:18 local time, a fire erupted within Dock Sector 7, rapidly spreading across multiple shipping containers. Emergency response units reported extreme heat concentration inconsistent with natural fire behavior. Despite widespread structural damage, several adjacent containers remained completely untouched. The fire self-extinguished after approximately six minutes. Post-incident inventory revealed a single container had been completely incinerated beyond material recovery. All other cargo remained intact.

KEY EVIDENCE

  • Residual flame patterns forming controlled burn boundaries
  • Heat intensity exceeding standard magical combustion thresholds
  • Traces of ignited arcane material within destroyed container
  • No ignition source identified

INVESTIGATOR NOTES

  • Uncontrolled destructive magic event
  • Fire spread avoided high-value shipments entirely
