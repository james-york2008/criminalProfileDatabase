STOLEN OBJECT

A fractured mirror shard embedded within a ceremonial altar piece. Cataloged as decorative damage.

INCIDENT SUMMARY

At approximately 01:57 local time, witnesses reported a sudden temperature drop followed by an intense flash of light. Contrary to previous incidents, the fire manifested after initial distortion, not before. The reliquary sustained minimal structural damage. However, the altar centerpiece was reduced to molten residue within seconds. Witnesses describe seeing “light bending inward” prior to ignition.

KEY EVIDENCE

  • Thermal inversion prior to combustion event
  • Arcane interference pattern overlapping flame signature
  • Residual energy inconsistent with Emberveil’s known profile
  • Fragmentary reflective particles exhibiting resonance decay

INVESTIGATOR NOTES

  • Subject demonstrates selective targeting
  • Fire used as secondary action, not primary method
  • Energy readings align with resonance signatures from XM-305 and XM-298
