Silver Market Disappearance

  • Case Number:
    #XM-305
  • Status:
    ACTIVE
  • Date:
    12/01/25
  • Location:
    Elaris Grand Market District
  • Time:
    09:15 PM
  • Witnesses:
    Multiple civilians

STOLEN OBJECT

An ornamental pendulum device sold as a novelty timepiece. Vendor records indicate it had remained unsold for over nine years.

INCIDENT SUMMARY

During peak market hours, multiple witnesses reported a “brief lapse” in awareness lasting approximately 4–8 seconds. Upon regaining awareness, a single vendor stall was found undisturbed, yet missing one item. No signs of struggle, forced entry, or environmental disruption were observed. Witness descriptions vary significantly, though several report seeing “light bending unnaturally” near the stall prior to the lapse.

KEY EVIDENCE

  • Concentrated lunar-aligned residue at point of disappearance
  • Minor temporal distortion recorded on nearby enchanted clocks
  • No physical traces recovered

INVESTIGATOR NOTES

  • Subject capable of operating in high-density civilian environments undetected
  • Increasing precision suggests familiarity with artifact identification
