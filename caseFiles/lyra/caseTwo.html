Phantom Treasury Incident

  • Case Number:
    #XM-298
  • Status:
    ACTIVE
  • Date:
    11/03/25
  • Location:
    Halcyon Central Treasury Annex
  • Time:
    01:42 AM
  • Witnesses:
    None

STOLEN OBJECT

A crystalline ledger prism used historically for ceremonial accounting rituals. Previously believed to hold no functional magical properties.

INCIDENT SUMMARY

At approximately 01:42 local time, internal treasury records began updating autonomously, logging transactions that did not occur. Moments later, all written and digital logs experienced partial data loss. Security review shows no physical breach. However, a six-second visual distortion event was detected across all surveillance systems. Following system restoration, a single archival artifact was confirmed missing. Notably, no currency reserves or high-value assets were affected.

KEY EVIDENCE

  • Residual arcane signature consistent with refractive illusion magic
  • Temporary data desynchronization across multiple storage systems
  • Silver particulate traces detected near archival vault (dissipated within minutes)

INVESTIGATOR NOTES

  • Subject demonstrates continued disinterest in monetary assets
  • Removed object emits low-level resonance under arcane scanning
