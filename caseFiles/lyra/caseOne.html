STOLEN OBJECT

A small, silver-inlaid prism mounted within a decorative housing. Previously classified as a ceremonial charm with no value, no threat designation, and no recorded strategic value.

INCIDENT SUMMARY

At approximately 02:17 local time, a silent breach occurred within Halcyon's Moonlit Vault, a secured facility housing various valuables. Security personnel reported a brief "disorientation event" that lasted around six to twelve seconds. During this event, all visual feeds experienced distortion consistent with illusionary magic. Only one item was confirmed missing.
